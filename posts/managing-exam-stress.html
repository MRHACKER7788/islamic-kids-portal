Managing Exam Stress

Home | About

Exams can be scary, but with the right attitude and a little duʿāʾ, you can do your best without fear.

1. Make a Study Plan

Break your revision into small tasks—just like breaking big goals into daily good deeds. Consistency wins over cramming.

2. Pray for Clarity

Before studying, recite a short duʿāʾ for knowledge (e.g., “Rabbi zidni ‘ilman”). This mind-set shift helps focus your heart and mind.

3. Take Breaks with Dhikr

During breaks, remember Allah by reciting “Subḥānallāh” 33 times. It refreshes you both mentally and spiritually.
