Dealing with School Bullying

Everyone gets teased sometimes—but Prophet Muḥammad ﷺ taught us to stand up with wisdom.

1. Talk to a Trusted Adult

Share your feelings with a teacher or parent—seeking help is a sign of strength.

2. Use Words, Not Fists

Calmly say, “Please stop, that hurts me.” The Qur’ān reminds us to respond to evil with good (Q 41:34).

3. Pray for Patience

A quick duʿāʾ for ṣabr (patience) can calm your heart and guide your response.
